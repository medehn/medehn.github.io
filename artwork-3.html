Studio Process

Insight into my creative journey — from conceptualization to finished piece. Every ceramic work tells a story of experimentation, technique refinement, and artistic intention.

Studio Location

Vienna, Austria

Primary Methods

Hand Building, Wheel Throwing

Materials

Stoneware & Porcelain

Kiln Type

Electric & Gas Kiln

My creative process begins with sketching ideas and exploring forms through clay. I work intuitively, allowing the material to guide the direction. Surface treatments are developed through repeated testing and refinement. The final pieces are fired in controlled kiln environments to achieve the desired color and texture. Each step in the process is essential to creating pieces that are both beautiful and functional.
