Striped Pattern

A hand-painted ceramic vessel featuring bold geometric striping. This piece combines traditional throwing techniques with contemporary surface design, creating a dynamic interplay between form and decoration.

Dimensions

20 cm H × 15 cm Diameter

Materials

Porcelain, Hand-Painted Glaze

Technique

Wheel Thrown, Hand Painted

Status

Available

This vessel demonstrates the balance between precision and spontaneity in ceramic work. The carefully planned striping pattern is executed by hand, giving each stroke a unique character while maintaining visual harmony. Perfect as a functional vase or standalone sculptural piece.
