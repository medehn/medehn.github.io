Studio Tools & Materials

The essential tools and materials that bring ceramic visions to life. From clay preparation to final finishing, each element plays a vital role in the creation process.

Clay Types

Stoneware, Porcelain, Earthenware

Tools

Throwing wheels, Hand sculpting tools

Glazes

Natural and commercial formulas

Firing

Cone 6 to Cone 10 temperatures

Quality materials and well-maintained tools are the foundation of good ceramic practice. I source clay from trusted suppliers and continuously experiment with glaze formulations to achieve unique color palettes. Proper tool care and studio organization ensure consistency and efficiency throughout the creative process.
