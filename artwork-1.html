Nature-Inspired Form

This sculptural ceramic piece draws inspiration from organic forms found in nature. The flowing curves and textured surface evoke the feeling of natural erosion and growth, combining functionality with artistic expression.

Dimensions

24 cm H × 18 cm W × 16 cm D

Materials

Stoneware Clay, Glazed Finish

Technique

Hand Building, Surface Texture

Status

Available

This piece explores the relationship between form and function, where the sculptural qualities serve both aesthetic and practical purposes. The surface treatment creates a tactile experience, inviting the viewer to engage with the work through touch.
